General Information

Our Study and Objective



In Our Study, as we investigated the diversity of birds in the site, and the correlation between bird diversity and the vegetation in the region, we found that the area had features that were underutilised.

With the creation of this website, we hope that this may help to raise public awareness and attract a larger pool of visitors.

Also, we aim to be cater to schools organising learning journeys of relevance to nature, as well as nature fanatics with vested interest in potential activities like bird-watching. We have thus designed relevant learning worksheets downloadable as pdf documents attached below, and created a video as you may see on the right panel.
